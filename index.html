The Answer Key Secret

Daniyal is a hardworking but average student, constantly pressured by his parents to achieve top grades. One day, he discovers what appears to be the final exam answer key in an empty classroom. Initially hesitant, he gives in to temptation, and his grades skyrocket. But soon, mysterious new answer keys appear before exams are even announced, revealing an impossible pattern. Daniyal discovers that someone is controlling the keys, predicting the future, and watching him closely. As he becomes dependent on this power, he faces guilt, fear, and the loss of his own sense of integrity. Ultimately, Daniyal must choose between easy success and staying true to himself. In the end, he takes exams honestly, realizing that self-respect and personal growth matter more than being number one.
